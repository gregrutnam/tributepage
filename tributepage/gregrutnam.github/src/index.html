Jurgen Klopp

The man who ended the 30 year wait.

Timeline of Jurgen's career

  • 1967 - Born in Stuttgart, West Germany
  • 1990- Begins his football career with FC Mainz 05
  • 1995 - Receives a diploma in sports science from Goethe University of Frankfurt
  • 2001 - Retires from playing professionally with Mainz and moves into coaching/managing them.
  • 2004- Gains promotion to the Bundesliga with Mainz for the first time in club history.
  • 2008 - Leaves Mainz to sign a contract with Borussia Dortmund eventually leading them to two Bundesliga titles (the last BL titles won by a non-Bayern Munich club as of writing), a Champions League final appearances as well as a handful of other domestic trophies.
  • 2015 - Signs with Liverpool FC
  • 2019 - Wins the UEFA Champions League with Liverpool FC
  • 2020- Wins the Premier League title with Liverpool FC.
